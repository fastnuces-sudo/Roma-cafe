Our Story

Aroma Café started as a small family project in 2015 with a simple vision: create a warm, welcoming place where people can enjoy quality coffee and real conversations.

Over the years, we have grown into a beloved neighborhood spot known for our handcrafted beverages, freshly baked pastries, and friendly staff. We carefully select our coffee beans, support local suppliers, and experiment with new recipes while staying true to our classic favorites.

Our Mission & Values

Quality First

From beans to baked goods, we never compromise on quality. Every cup and every bite is prepared with care.

Community

We aim to be more than just a café. We host small events, support local artists, and create space for everyone.

Sustainability

We reduce waste, use recyclable materials where possible, and partner with environmentally conscious suppliers.
